Let's Grow Together

We love what we do, and we love the people we work with. We are a bunch of engineers, artists, photographers, and craftsmen that want to make a dent in this world. Together, we can shape the future of how creatives run their businesses online.

VIEW OPENINGS

Small Team. Big Impact.

The Pixieset team is a small, tight-knit group of talented individuals. No matter if you are a developer, designer, or customer support expert, we all work together for the greater good of Pixieset and our customers. We all share the same passion for creating beautiful and simple-to-use products. Every team member wears multiple hats to solve tough problems every day.

Craftsmanship Matters

Good things take time. We believe that the length of the design process is whatever time it takes to deliver exactly what is needed. We take a lot of pride in our product because we spend a significant amount of time to design, build, test, and re-test every feature until we feel that it exceeds our standards.

Make a difference.

We strive to make an impact and leave a legacy as a team. We stay humble with our craft but it feels good to be recognized as well!

Work Hard. Play Hard.

Many of us enjoy the art of coffee, craft beer, beach volleyball, hiking, shooting weddings and more. We encourage everyone to maintain a healthy work-life balance and to "work hard, play hard."

Perks & Benefits

We offer a range of benefits and perks to make sure that our team members are taken care of both at work and at home.

Comprehensive Benefits

A well rounded health, dental, and vision care benefits plan for you and your family.

Four Weeks Vacation

Every team member gets four weeks of paid time off to take vacations and recharge..

Home Office Setup Budget

A one-time budget to build out your workspace for the times that you’re working remotely.

Wellness Allowance

An annual allowance to spend on health and wellness. Whether it’s a gym or yoga membership, half-marathon races, or transit passes, choose what’s best for you.

Education Allowance

Interested in a course, attending a conference, or buying a book? We've got you covered with an annual education spending budget.

Donation Matching

You have causes you care about, and we want to help your contributions go that much further.
