Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free account work?

The Pixieset Free account gives you access to most of the features of all our apps: Client Gallery, Website, Studio Manager, Store and Mobile Gallery App. There are no setup fees, no credit card required. Only upgrade when you need to.

How long are your contracts?

Pixieset subscriptions are paid monthly or yearly. You can switch or cancel your service at any time.

Do you offer discounts for yearly service?

Yes, we offer a discounted rate on all annual subscription plans.

Can I switch plans?

Yes, you can change your subscription plan at any time. When changing your plan, your previous plan will be prorated and applied to the cost of your new plan.

How do I cancel service?

You can cancel your subscription at any time directly from your account dashboard.

Is there a commission fee on my store sales?

There are no commission fees for all upgraded accounts. Free accounts are subject to a 15% commission fee on store sales.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes. You can use your own domain name for your website and client galleries on any of our upgraded accounts. We also provide a free mypixieset.com domain to all accounts upon signup.

Do I need a web host?

No. All Pixieset accounts include secure and reliable hosting for your website and galleries..

What type of payments do you support?

We currently accept Visa, MasterCard credit and debit cards, as well as American Express credit cards. All payments are billed in USD.

Have more questions?

Our support team is available to help. Visit our Help & Support Center to contact them.
